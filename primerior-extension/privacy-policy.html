Privacy Policy for Primerior

Effective Date:

Primerior ("we", "us", or "our") operates the Primerior Chrome Extension (the "Extension"). This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Extension.

1. Information Collection and Use

We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting it. The Extension is designed to prioritize user privacy.

Personal Data (Google Account)

Our Extension uses the "identity" permission to allow you to sign in with your Google Account. This is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Authenticating you as a user.
  • Syncing your saved content (notes, collections, etc.) across your devices.

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal identification information to others.

Content Data

The core function of Primerior is to help you capture and organize text and media. This data is:

  • Stored securely.
  • Accessible only by you.
  • Used strictly to provide the features of the Extension (displaying your collections, searching your notes).

2. Permissions

To function, the Extension requires specific permissions:

  • Storage: To save your preferences and cached data locally.
  • Identity: To authenticate with Google services.
  • ActiveTab/ContextMenus: To allow you to clip content from the current page you are viewing.

3. Third-Party Services

We use Google OAuth 2.0 for authentication. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for more information on how they handle your data.

4. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
